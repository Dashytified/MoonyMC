Information: This plugin aims at introducing more uses for the regular mushrooms by overriding default game mechanics introducing unique reactivity at the hand of specific interactivity. Eat, grow, cook or snort mushrooms, be free. This plugin will also allow me to touch upon mycology, as some details will have to be accurate. The plugin will grow as time moves on, for now, the aim is to make edible mushrooms a thing.
